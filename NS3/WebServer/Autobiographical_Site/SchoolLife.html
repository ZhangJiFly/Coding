School Life

London Nursery School

I remember almost nothing about london nursery school, Karen, Siri, Naya and the face of a nameless black kid. But I did take with me a leaving book made for me by the good people called "Amber cat is Searching for Joshua" (who can blame it) nostalgia or what.

Hatfield Peverel Nursery

My memories of this consist more or less of playing with paint, that was my main pass-time, nap time oh what a great time that was and getting beaten up by ben and a group of girls well unless my tricycle skills were particularly good that day.

Hatfield Peverel Infants School

This was a fun filled time of eating glue and rubbers with Beth Patterson, a broken arm (yes thats right also a tree related injury) with my mum coming in to force me to write with my left hand whilst other people were falling asleep in assembly, but thats mum for you, and last but not least being a little shit as i was friends with Peter, Tony and Tom.

Hatfield Peverel Junior School

Well although my memory is a lot clearer for this time I wont bore you with the details. Again mostly spent being a badest ass little shit in school other than our leader Tony Rowe, I stopped being friends with Tom because he was spending too much time with Zoe his girlfriend (lol he was 7!) we didn't speak for a whole year! In year 4 I was put into special english classes because I was behind mostly because I didn't give enough of a shit to try.
